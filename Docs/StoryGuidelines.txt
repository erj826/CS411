User Stories

1. Target Users

Students who have access to Spotify and who have a competitive spirit.

2. Product description
Fantasy sports meets music. Listen Up. 


3. Product Logic 
User logs in. Can view their Facebook friends who also have the app. Choose to challenge their friends. 
Can pick genre or generate a random genre. Then choose songs to add to their lineup. Competitors cannot
see their competition's lineup until they have finished choosing their own. After n amount of time, 
winner is announced based on number of Spotify listens. 

4. I/O
User selects a 'roster' of n songs. 
App outputs total listens over the course of a week.
